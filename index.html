About us

Unicxa Software is a leading software company that specializes in Java development. Our team of experts is dedicated to creating innovative and efficient solutions for businesses of all sizes. Our contributions to Java Development have been significant, with our advanced tools and technologies helping to streamline the development process and enhance overall performance. We continuously strive to stay updated with the latest trends and advancements in Java, ensuring that our clients receive the highest quality software solutions. Our commitment to excellence has earned us a reputation as a trusted partner for businesses looking to elevate their Java development capabilities.

Unicxa Software Organization was founded in 2012 by our founder Kevin C, with a vision to provide innovative and cutting-edge software solutions to businesses and organizations. From our initial inception, we have been dedicated to developing open source software, allowing for collaboration and accessibility for our clients. We have also been at the forefront of the Java development industry, utilizing this powerful programming language to create robust and efficient software for our clients. With a strong focus on quality and customer satisfaction, we have grown and evolved over the years, establishing ourselves as a leading software organization in the industry.

Kevin C

Kevin C is the head developer for Unicxa Software Organization, a position he has held for the past five years. With over a decade of experience in the field of software development, Kevin brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to his role. He is known for his strong leadership skills and his ability to inspire and motivate his team to deliver high-quality products. Kevin is also highly skilled in project management, ensuring that all projects are completed on time and within budget. His dedication and passion for his work have played a key role in the success of Unicxa Software Organization, making him an invaluable asset to the company.
